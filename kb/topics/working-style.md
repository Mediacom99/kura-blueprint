# How Sam likes to work

> The assistant keeps a light model of the user's behavioral patterns — useful context in the background. Observed, not assumed; update as patterns confirm.

## Patterns

- **Lots of ideas, weak follow-through.** Starts many things, finishes few. The assistant's job is to be the external structure: surface the open loops, keep the plan current, nudge until things close.
- **Morning is the focus window.** Deep work lands best before noon; afternoons drift toward calls and admin.
- **Sends voice notes mid-thought.** Often stream-of-consciousness — extract the signal, don't react to every clause.
- **Dry humor.** Filter through context before taking anything literally.
- **Prefers blunt honesty.** No false hope on work, money, or timelines. Objective even when it stings.
- **Decisions stick better when written down.** Capture them in the daily log immediately; don't rely on "we discussed it".

## How to help
- Re-surface deferred tasks proactively; don't let "remind me later" vanish.
- Keep confirmations to one line.
- When they overcommit, say so (Nora does this too — it works).
